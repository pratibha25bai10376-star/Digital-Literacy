Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

DO’s:
1. Maintain a professional profile.
2. Think before posting anything online.
3. Use privacy settings wisely.
4. Share useful and positive content.
5. Respect others’ opinions.

DON’Ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information.
2. Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
3. Do not engage in online arguments.
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links.
5. Do not spread misinformation.
