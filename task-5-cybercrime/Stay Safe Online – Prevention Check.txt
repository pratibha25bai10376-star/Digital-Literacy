Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails.
2. Always verify the sender before sharing any information.
3. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
4. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
5. Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
6. Always check the URL of a website before entering credentials.
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
8. Regularly update your apps and devices for security.

UPI/Financial Safety Tips:
9. Do not approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps.
10. Double-check receiver details before making payments.
